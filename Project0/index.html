Project 0: Becoming Friends with Your Camera

Overview

This project explores how cameras capture perspective, focal length, and projection through hands-on experimentation. I captured a close and distant portrait of myself, a street roadblock from different distances, and a dolly zoom of a Snorlax plushie to see how distance and zoom change the appearance of a scene. The result is a series of images that visually demonstrate these effects.
